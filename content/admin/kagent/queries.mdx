The **Queries** page in *KAgent* hosts a running log of all queries
made against the database.


![../images/queries.png](/content/admin/kagent/images/queries.png)


## Actions


The available actions on the **Queries** page are as follows:

* Refresh the list of queries by clicking **Refresh**
* Update any of the **Advanced Filter** fields and click
  **Apply Filter** to filter the queries list based on the
  provided values. Click **Clear** to remove the filter.
* Open a detailed view of a query by clicking **Detail** next to the
  desired query:


![../images/queries_details.png](/content/admin/kagent/images/queries_details.png)
