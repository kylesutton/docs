title: User
---

Click your username in the top navigation menu to review open *GAdmin* sessions,
change your password, or log out of *GAdmin*.


## Sessions


Click **Sessions** to open the list of currently open *GAdmin* sessions.
Select any of the sessions not designated as **Current** (displays the
![current](/content/admin/images/current_session.png) logo) and click **Terminate** to remotely terminate that
session. The user for the session will be forced to login again.


![../../images/admin_sessions.png](/content/admin/images/admin_sessions.png)


## Change Password


Click **Change Password** to change your password.  The new password
must meet the password strength requirements listed.  Click **Save** to
confirm the password change.


![../../images/admin_changepassword.png](/content/admin/images/admin_changepassword.png)


## Logout


Click **Logout** to end the current *GAdmin* session and return to the
login page.
